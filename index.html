Visit Naples

Enjoy history, culture, and stunning seaside views in the vibrant city of Naples.

Top three activities to do in Naples

Explore Pompeii

Walk through ancient streets, villas, and frescoes frozen in time by Mount Vesuvius' eruption.

Try Neapolitan pizza

Enjoy the world-famous Margherita or Marinara, baked to perfection in a wood-fired oven.

Stroll along the lungomare

Take in stunning sea views, with Mount Vesuvius as a breathtaking backdrop.

Day Trips.

Day 1: Capri

  • Visit the Blue Grotto and Monte Solaro.
  • Stroll through the Piazzetta.
  • Take a boat tour around the island.

Day 2: The Amalfi Coast

  • Explore Positano, Amalfi and Ravello.
  • Enjoy scenic coastal views.
  • Try fresh seafood.

Day 3: Phleghraean Fields

  • See volcanic craters and Roman ruins.
  • Visit Lake Avernus, linked to mythology.
  • Explore the ancient city of Cumae.
